Doc-the-Bot

I'm your Personal Virtual Doctor

What Am I?

I'm a set of python codes which gives me the power to compute complex relationships between various diseases and their symptoms. And hence facilitating the user to be aware of his health issues.

Where To Find Me?


Robots are rapidly finding their way in our day to day life. From automation to minimizing human errors, bots have proved themselves of invaluable worth.

Therefore, we decided why not use this code-marvel to do something in Health and Medicine?! After all, health comes first and prevention is better than cure.

Our Bot is an interactive way of making people aware of their health issues in early stages. It uses the power of computing to analyse symptoms accurately and further refer to a doctor who expertise in detected health hazard, if any.
